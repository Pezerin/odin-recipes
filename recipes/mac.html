Mac and Cheese

Description

Pasta shells made in a cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Shell Pasta
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Melt butter, add flour and whisk until golden brown
  3. Add milk and seasoning
  4. Cook until able to coat back of spoon
  5. Add cheese slowly and then add pasta
  6. Serve
